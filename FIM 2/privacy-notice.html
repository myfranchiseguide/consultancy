Privacy Notice

Effective Date: January 2026

MYFG / MYFranchiseGuide respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 of Malaysia and other applicable personal data protection requirements.

This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal data when you submit a form, download a checklist, book a consultation, make payment, attend a consultation session, or communicate with us.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

  • full name;
  • email address;
  • phone number or WhatsApp number;
  • company name, if applicable;
  • job title, business background, or franchise interest, if provided;
  • franchise brands or business opportunities you are considering, if provided;
  • consultation package selected;
  • booking date, time, and session details;
  • payment confirmation, transaction reference, invoice details, and billing information;
  • messages, enquiries, form responses, and consultation-related information;
  • website usage data, cookies, tracking data, and interaction data where applicable.

We only collect personal data that is necessary or relevant for the purposes stated in this Privacy Notice.

2. How We Collect Personal Data

We may collect personal data when you:

  • submit a form on our website or landing page;
  • download a checklist, guide, or resource;
  • book a consultation session;
  • make payment for a consultation package;
  • communicate with us through email, WhatsApp, phone, social media, or other channels;
  • attend an event, consultation, webinar, or coaching session;
  • interact with our website, ads, emails, or digital platforms.

3. Purpose of Collecting and Processing Personal Data

We may collect, use, and process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • to respond to your enquiry;
  • to send you the requested checklist, guide, or resource;
  • to manage consultation bookings and session scheduling;
  • to process payments and issue invoices or receipts;
  • to assign consultants and prepare for consultation sessions;
  • to send booking confirmations, reminders, updates, and follow-up messages;
  • to provide consultation, coaching, education, or strategic guidance services;
  • to keep internal records for administrative, accounting, tax, legal, or compliance purposes;
  • to improve our services, landing pages, communications, and customer experience;
  • to send marketing communications, event updates, educational content, or offers where you have given consent or where permitted by law;
  • to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests from authorities.

4. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose your personal data where necessary to:

  • MYFG / MYFranchiseGuide team members;
  • assigned consultants, including Claudia, Shamsul, Alfred, or other authorised consultants;
  • booking and scheduling platforms;
  • CRM, email, automation, and communication platforms;
  • payment processors, banks, or financial service providers;
  • accounting, tax, legal, compliance, or professional advisors;
  • IT, website hosting, analytics, and data storage service providers;
  • government authorities, regulators, or enforcement bodies where required by law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you franchise education content, event updates, offers, resources, and marketing communications by email, WhatsApp, SMS, phone call, social media, or other communication channels.

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or using any unsubscribe or opt-out method provided in our messages.

Opting out of marketing communications does not affect essential service-related communications, such as booking confirmations, payment matters, invoices, consultation reminders, or follow-up relating to a service you requested.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website or landing pages may use cookies, pixels, analytics tools, CRM tracking, or similar technologies to understand website usage, improve user experience, measure campaign performance, and support advertising or remarketing activities.

You may adjust your browser settings to block or manage cookies. However, some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no internet transmission, website, email, or digital storage system is completely secure. You acknowledge that any information submitted online is provided at your own risk.

8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes stated in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

As a general guide:

  • enquiry and lead data may be retained for up to 24 months;
  • consultation, booking, invoice, and payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for accounting, tax, legal, or administrative purposes;
  • marketing contact data may be retained until you withdraw consent, unsubscribe, or request deletion, subject to legal or business record requirements.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may contact us to:

  • request access to your personal data;
  • request correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal data;
  • withdraw consent to the processing of your personal data, where applicable;
  • opt out of direct marketing communications;
  • ask questions about how your personal data is collected, used, stored, or disclosed.

Please note that withdrawing consent may affect our ability to provide certain services, process bookings, issue invoices, or respond to enquiries.

10. Accuracy of Personal Data

You are responsible for ensuring that the personal data you provide to us is accurate, complete, and up to date.

If your personal data changes, please notify us so that we can update our records.

11. Third-Party Links and Platforms

Our website, landing pages, emails, or messages may contain links to third-party websites, booking platforms, payment processors, or external tools.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of third-party platforms. You should review their privacy notices before submitting personal data to them.

12. Updates to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or data processing practices.

The latest version will be made available on our website or landing page.
