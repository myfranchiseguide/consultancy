Why I'm organizing this

Nothing beats real human connection in an AI-driven world.

Many of you have asked for an offline, in-person event. Somewhere to learn how to actually evaluate a franchise business and connect with other people doing the same homework. So this is the first of its kind. I'm bringing the industry experts and the brands into one intimate setting, half a day, for us to connect properly.

Most franchise events here optimise for footfall. Brands pay big to be on stage, attendees show up for free food, and the actual conversations (the candid ones, the careful ones) happen quietly in a corner.

The Franchise Insider is built differently. The room is intentionally small, so the conversations can be real. The brand pitches are bounded, so you don't sit through hours of pitches dressed up as content. Industry authorities and a lender are in the room, because that's where the real decision-making happens. And the ticket price is deliberately low (RM 99) so money isn't the filter. Seriousness is.

If you're thinking about franchising as a real path, come and ask the hard questions in this room. It's much cheaper than learning them the expensive way later.

Who this is for

This is for you if

  • You're wondering if franchising is a real route to creating generational wealth.
  • You're seriously considering investing in a franchise, but you're not sure what's actually out there.
  • You'd love to connect with other like-minded people, hear what they've found, and learn from their perspectives.
  • You want to explore passive income options that aren't another property or another stock pick.
  • You're sick of your 9 to 5 and looking for a way out, or you're considering another vertical to build wealth.
  • You want transparency, want to make a smart decision, and want to walk into a deal with full confidence.

This is not for you if

  • You want someone to tell you which specific franchise to buy.
  • You want guaranteed returns. We don't sell those, and you should be careful of anyone who does.
  • You're looking for a free meet and greet with brands. There's no free coffee here, only real conversations.
  • You're not willing to ask questions. The value of this room is unlocked by the questions you bring.
  • You're here to pitch your services or network for sales leads. This isn't that kind of room.
  • You think franchising is a get-rich-quick game. It's a real business that operators take years to build.

FAQ
Is this a sales pitch in disguise?

There are live brand pitches on the agenda, and we're upfront about that. They're one of five sessions and bounded to under two hours. The rest of the day is independent. A lender giving sector-level guidance. And really kind-hearted operators sharing their experience. You won't be cornered.

I'm not sure if franchising is for me yet. Should I come?

Yes. By learning about the businesses and the perspectives in the room, you'll make a much more informed decision about whether this is for you. Worst case scenario, you walk out with a clearer view of what it takes to build a real business. That's useful whether you're already a business owner or thinking of becoming one.

Why is the ticket so cheap?

The event is funded by partner brands and sponsors. The RM 99 ticket isn't paying for the room. It's making sure the room is full of people who actually want to be there. Free events fill with people who don't show up. We chose against that.

Are the brands and partners vetted, or is it pay to play?

Vetted. We've turned down brands we didn't think the room would benefit from. The partners pay for the platform. They don't pay for our endorsement.

I'm a current franchisee. Is this for me?

Yes, if you're considering scaling, switching, or you just want a clearer read on where the industry is heading. You'll also likely be useful in the lunch-table conversations.

What if I sign up and can't make it?

Sorry, tickets are final. But you can transfer your seat to someone you trust. Just let us know who's coming in your place before the day.

Will the sessions be recorded?

No. The sessions are off the record by design. That's what allows the operators and the lender to speak candidly. You're paying for the room, not the replay.

Can I bring a colleague or partner?

Yes, especially if you're thinking about buying a franchise as a family or a group of friends. All the stakeholders should be aligned before any money moves. But the cap is real. We can't accommodate more than 50 people. Once it's full, we'll close registration.
